"Inori":https://github.com/inori-lang is a programming language project by a friend of mine. It is really more of an experiment than an actual language, because it implements a lot of novel concepts and the specification is constantly changing.

For the logo and identity, we wanted something simple and powerful, with a japanese vibe. Something that expressed the design goals of the language, as well as the project's optimistic, excited outlook.

You can check out the complete brand identity guidelines "here":https://speakerdeck.com/bertob/inori-brand-book.
